RANGE ROVER COLOURS

An Adventure Waiting to Happen!

Land Rover’s Range Rover is one of the most iconic cars on the road, with a history going back to 1970! So how does it manage to look so fresh and exciting, 50 years into production?

Clever engineering and regular design refreshes no doubt have something to do with so many of us putting the Range Rover at the top of our dream car list, year after year. The striking Range Rover colour palette is just the icing on the cake.

There’s choice of 13 different Range Rover colours in solid, metallic or premium metallic finish, each named after a mountain, valley or river. Each region is noted for its beauty, inspiration or innovation, much like the Range Rover itself.

Simple Solids

Let’s face it, the Land Rover Range Rover has enough to attract admiring looks whatever the colour. So if you like to keep things simple, the Solid Range Rover colours could be for you.

Land Rover calls these ‘flat’ colours, but you’ll stand out a mile whether you choose the strong and sleek Narvik Black* or the pure and clean Fuji White which both come as standard on all models.

Marvellous Metallics

Yearning for something with a little more sparkle? Range Rover Metallics deliver an eye-catching finish in a range of colours from cool silver to fiery red, at no extra cost.

Named after one of the longest rivers in Asia, Indus has incredible staying power that goes for miles and miles, just like the Range Rover.
